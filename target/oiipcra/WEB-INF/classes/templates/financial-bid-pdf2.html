6.0   Computation of Bid Capacity of the lowest responsive bidder

6.1
The bid capacity of bidder is computed following the limitations provided in ITB Clause -3 (b) for amount of Annual Financial Turnover and Clause-3 (g) for Availability of liquid assets and/or credit facilities. Further, according to clause-3, when a bidder participates in multiple bids, the bidder has to demonstrate the qualifications for Annual Turnover (3(b)) and Availability of liquid assets (3(g)) to the cumulative value of the packages. Further, the balance bid capacity after award of work is computed as under:
Maximum Bid Capacity = 1 x Max Annual Turnover x Period for Completion in Years. Balance Bid Capacity = Max Bid Capacity - 2 x Estd. Cost of Work
